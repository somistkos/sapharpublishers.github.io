How It Works

Make Package Selection

Clients should go through our packages and pick the service that match their needs.

Contact Us

Clients should contact us either through mail or phone to discuss their preference. We will work hand in hand with them to ensure they make the right decision regarding their books. After agreeing on all terms, a working plan would be drawn based on customer needs.

Make Payment

A proposal form would be sent to clients based on the outcome of step two. After receiving the form, monetary payment is expected to be made by clients through our payment channels.

Fill Form

Clients are expected to fill the form and return a copy back to us for our financial team to process it. (Note: the form would only be processed after our team has been notified of payment).

Receive A Client Service Code

A service code would be sent to clients through mail or text message for clients to use to access/receive feedback on their work. (Note: this would be so after the form has been processed)

Send Your Manuscript

Clients would now send their manuscripts to us through our email for our team to begin work. (Note: the email should contain client service code for easy identification and sorting.)

Work on Manuscript

That is all!. Our team would do the necessary work.

Note To Clients

Clients are required to give out valid phone number(s) and email address. This will be used to contact them if we have any question regarding their manuscript

Charges will differ for different book sizes and books that have pages beyond 150

Payments are meant to be made in full before commencement of work. Or a 60% deposit with balance meant to be paid before we move to the design stage.

Once book is finalize, clients can print more copies, as little as 10 books to as many as they need at a highly competitive price.

Individuals whose rating do not fall within 5-10 of our book rating scale would not get their books published by us. (We stand for quality, therefore, we don’t just publish any kind of book, we publish books which have enough value and would benefit readers)
