About Us

Saphar is a self-publishing company that offers high-class publishing services to authors worldwide. We have the tools to transform the manuscripts of authors into products that sell. We will work hand in hand with you so as to get the very best for your book. Our packages are well designed to meet your needs. No matter your financial situation, you can have a book written in your name. If you want a publishing company that would listen to you and ensure you get a lot from your book, then Saphar publishers is all you need.

Vision

Changing the world through writing.

Mission

As a company, our mission is to bring writers closer to readers, and readers closer to writers and to make book writing a marathon, not a sprint.

Company Values
